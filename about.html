Short Bio:

My name is Aitana, a 17 years old teenager born in La Habana Cuba, in November 19th of 2000. From a young age, I have been moving all around, I lived in the Basque country and in Madrid, and right now I'm in Miami.

Artists Statement:

I love all forms of art and I personally like variety, so I work with a great variety of mediums. But, I consider myself a painter, my pieces are mostly full of color.
